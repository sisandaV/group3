Editorial
Welcome to the Cape Kidney Association's first newsletter. The Cape Kidney Association is a Grassroots Non-Profit Organisation that raises funds for indigent kidney patients at the various Provincial Hospitals in and around the greater Western Cape. With this newsletter, we intend to make it a regular publication and to use it to keep you in touch with news and activities which relate to the association.

World Kidney Day sunset 5km walk. "Kidney Health for all"
The Cape Kidney Association in partnership with the City of Cape Town hosted their 5th annual Awareness Sunset 5km walk in support of world Kidney Day. World Kidney Day is a campaign that was established to raise awareness and to educate the public about caring for their kidneys. It is held every year on the second Thursday of March. The day turned out to be a successful sunset walk and was enjoyed by all who participated. Thanks to all our sponsors and supporters for their continued generosity. We would also like to thanks the participants and volenteers for helping to make this event success.
